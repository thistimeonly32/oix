Inform Decam before and after Fire Alarm maintainance.
Inform supervising authority and isolate interlocking output zones before testing.
Check the main ‘On’ LED light is lit up.
Check the condition and voltage of standby batteries under load (disconnect charger).
Check all printed circuit boards are secured in their mountings and examine for sign of over-heating and damaged tracks, dry joints etc.
Check all ribbon cables and cable terminations are secured and correctly.
Physical check on all manual call points and break glass for damaged cause by knocks, vandalism.
Function Test: To test and activate smoke/heat detector.Ensure all alarm signals are transmitted to the Main Fire Alarm Panel .
Inform supervising authority and get permission to test all output devices
Physical check and test all alarm bells.
Check and test all alarm sirens if any.
Check remote signals for lift homing, door activation, pressurizing fans etc. are in working order when system is on alarm.
Check and ensure that the alarm signals are received at Central.
Check that system is left fully operational and clean before leaving premises.
