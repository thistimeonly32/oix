DRAINING OF SPRINKLER/HOSEREEL SYSTEM
Check PERMIT TO WORK is approved by Owner and Main Contractor.
Inform Decam before and after draining and recharging work.
Inform supervising authority and isolate interlocking output zones before work commence.
Brief and coordinate with Chubb/other contractor before commence draining.
Check and ensure basic Personal Pretective Equipment for all workers.
Proceed with Authorize person to the control valve location and confirm working area.
Unlock the control valve room, unlock control valve and cv drain valve.
Proceed to drain control valve until pressure indicate Zero.psi.
DISMANTLE OF FIRST SPRINKLER HEAD OR PIPES.
Proceed to work area and ensure floors and equipments protection.
Check & ensure again the control valve drain is for the actual work area.
Turn off nozzle.
Proceed to drain Balance water from pipe by opening sprinkler heads or pipes.
If there is a pressure switch connected at control valve, then check at Fire Alarm Panel for any alarm signal received.
Proceed with the pipe work and ensure safety at all time.
RECHARGE OF SPRINKLER/HOSEREEL SYSTEM
Check Exinguisher pressure gauge reading is correct within the green sector and that the safety pin is intact. Do not remove the safety pin until required, as it has been designed to break on removal.
Visual check the Fire Extiguisher if it does not have any corrosion.
Heft the cylinder and refill content if necessary.
Check the Fire Extinguisher if Dry/Powder by overturning it.
Remove the existing Fire Extinguisher label.
Normalization of Sprinkler/Hosereel system
Check and ensure sprinkler pressure is maintain. Proceed to the work site and check no leak on new and existing pipe work.
Maintain a-half hour'Cooling' period before normalizing sprinkler/Hosereel system to normal operational order.
Check that all associated gate valves are strapped and locked.
Normalize all interlocking output zones after work and inform Decam of wotk completion.
